About

Contrast is the force that separates elements so they can be seen. It gives priority and invites interpretation. This page reflects on contrast as a concept and demonstrates practical ways to apply it in web design.

Contrast in design

Color & Tone

Differences in luminance and color make text legible and shapes distinct. Good color contrast improves readability for everyone.

Accent on white

Scale & Weight

Size and typographic weight set hierarchy: headlines vs. body, bold vs. regular. These contrasts guide attention.

Space & Texture

Negative space, texture, and borders create separation and emphasis without extra color.
